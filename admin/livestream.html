How to Go Live

  1. Start streaming on YouTube Studio or the YouTube app.
  2. Copy your live URL from YouTube (e.g., https://youtube.com/live/abc123).
  3. Paste the URL in the field above and toggle the status to Live.
  4. Save settings — the home page will immediately show the live embed.
  5. After the stream ends, toggle the status back to Offline and save.
