About Us

Preserving the Legacy of Karbala

Bharat Web Azadari is a dedicated digital media platform committed to preserving and broadcasting the sacred traditions of Shia Azadari. Founded in Senthal, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, we serve the global Shia community by providing live and recorded coverage of Majlis, Juloos, Nohay programs, and Muharram events.

Our mission is to ensure that the teachings of Karbala and the message of Imam Hussain (A.S.) reach every corner of the world through modern digital media.
