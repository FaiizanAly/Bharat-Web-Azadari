Our Story

Bharat Web Azadari

Bharat Web Azadari is a dedicated digital media platform committed to preserving and broadcasting the sacred traditions of Shia Azadari.

Founded in Senthal, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, we serve the global Shia community by providing live and recorded coverage of Majlis, Juloos, Nohay programs, and Muharram events. Our mission is to ensure that the teachings of Karbala and the message of Imam Hussain (A.S.) reach every corner of the world through modern digital media.

Through our YouTube channel and this website, we strive to connect the Shia community — whether local or worldwide — by making Azadari accessible to all. We believe that the message of Karbala is timeless and must continue to be shared through every available medium.

Our team works tirelessly during Muharram and throughout the year to document and broadcast every Majlis, Juloos, and Nohay program, ensuring high-quality coverage that honors the sanctity of these events.

Mission & Vision

The values that guide everything we do

🎯

Our Mission

To provide authentic, high-quality Azadari content to the global Shia community. We are committed to live and recorded coverage of Majlis, Juloos, and Nohay programs, ensuring that the message of Imam Hussain (A.S.) and the teachings of Karbala remain accessible to all believers — regardless of their location.

  • ✦ Live broadcasts of Majlis programs
  • ✦ High-quality Nohay recordings
  • ✦ Complete Juloos procession coverage
  • ✦ Muharram event documentation
🌟

Our Vision

To become the leading digital platform for Shia Azadari in India and beyond. We envision a future where every believer, no matter where they are in the world, can participate in the sacred traditions of Azadari through our digital platform — keeping the flame of Karbala burning bright.

  • ✦ Premier Azadari platform in India
  • ✦ Reaching every corner of the world
  • ✦ Preserving Azadari for generations
  • ✦ Building a global Shia community
